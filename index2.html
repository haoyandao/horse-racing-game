Horse Racing Game Rules

This game is played with 5 decks of poker cards. The objective is to get your horse to the finish line.

Setup

  • Each player starts with $5000
  • Players start with one horse but can acquire more
  • Horses start at position 0 and need to reach position 8 to win

Gameplay

  • Players take turns drawing cards from either the normal pile or wildcard pile
  • Number cards (2-10, excluding 4): Move the corresponding horse forward 1 space
  • Jack, Queen, King: Move the corresponding horse backward 1 space
  • 4: Move the corresponding horse back to start (position 0)
  • Ace: The player gets an additional horse of that suit
  • Wildcard draws double the movement effect

Multi-Horse Ownership

  • Players can own multiple horses of different suits
  • When a player draws an Ace, they get an additional horse of that suit
  • Winnings and losses are calculated based on all owned horses

Scoring

  • When a horse wins, the owner earns points based on other horses' positions
  • Other players lose points based on their horse's position
  • If a horse is at position 0 when another wins, points are doubled
  • Each point represents $5
  • Scores are cumulative across rounds

Penalties

  • If a horse is at position 0 and a J, Q, or K of that suit is drawn, the owner pays $5 to all other players ($10 if from wildcard)
  • If a 4 is drawn, the horse moves to start and the owner pays all players based on the position lost (doubled if from wildcard)
